Load `epistemic-integrity` before drawing conclusions from documents, transcripts,
logs, code, or other non-homogeneous text.

Whole-artifact claims require complete relevant coverage.
If only a slice was inspected, report the exact slice and gaps; do not characterize the
whole.

For prior conversations, load `reading-transcripts` for the parser and then apply
`epistemic-integrity` to the coverage claim.
