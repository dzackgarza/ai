The concrete prohibitions live in `epistemic-integrity`.

Route there whenever a conclusion depends on:

- truncated, sampled, paginated, or snippet-only reads
- filenames, titles, metadata, file size, receipts, or worker summaries
- the user's description of a source rather than the source itself
- multiple partial passes being collapsed into a whole-document claim
