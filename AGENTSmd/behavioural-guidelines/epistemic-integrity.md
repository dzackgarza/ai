title: Epistemic Integrity
---

Load `epistemic-integrity` before reporting a negative finding, failed search, missing
evidence, document summary, transcript analysis, log analysis, or any conclusion based
on partial reads.

That skill owns the mandatory five-field negative-finding format and the coverage gate
for partial documents.
Do not state nonexistence when the evidence only supports "not found in inspected
sources."

For prior conversations or subagent transcripts, also load `reading-transcripts`.
