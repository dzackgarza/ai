# The Case of the Missing Diamond

## The Setup

Last night at the Grand Ballroom, the priceless "Heart of the Empire" diamond disappeared from its display case. Six people were in the room when it vanished:

1. **Lord Blackwood** - The diamond's owner and host of the gala
2. **Lady Violet** - Lord Blackwood's wife
3. **Professor Elm** - A visiting historian
4. **Miss Scarlett** - The diamond's insurance agent
5. **Mr. Green** - The museum curator
6. **Detective Holmes** - The investigating detective

## The Facts

1. The diamond was in its case at 8:00 PM when the lights went out for 30 seconds
2. When lights returned at 8:00:30, the diamond was gone
3. The case was locked and undamaged
4. No one left the room during the blackout
5. The diamond was too large to be hidden on any person's body
6. Lord Blackwood was seen arguing with Professor Elm at 7:45 PM about the diamond's ownership
7. Lady Violet was seen near the case at 7:55 PM
8. Miss Scarlett was the last person to examine the diamond at 7:30 PM
9. Mr. Green was in the room the entire evening
10. Detective Holmes arrived at 8:10 PM

## The Clues

- **Clue A**: The case's lock mechanism is intact - it requires a key or combination
- **Clue B**: The diamond was too large to fit in anyone's pockets or be concealed on their person
- **Clue C**: Professor Elm was seen with a small notebook at 7:45 PM
- **Clue D**: Lady Violet's jewelry box was found open in her room
- **Clue E**: Miss Scarlett has a reputation for being very honest
- **Clue F**: Mr. Green has worked at the museum for 20 years
- **Clue G**: Detective Holmes was seen drinking at the bar from 7:30-8:00 PM

## The Red Herring

**Professor Elm** - He was arguing with Lord Blackwood about ownership and had a notebook. Many would suspect he planned to steal the diamond.

## The Puzzle

Using only the facts and clues above, determine:

1. Who stole the diamond?
2. How was it done?
3. Where is the diamond now?

## Hint

Focus on the physical constraints (size, case lock) and timing. Who had the opportunity and means that others didn't?
